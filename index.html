Agriculture Awareness Programs for Farmers

A digital initiative to bridge the gap between farmers and agriculture officers.

Topics: Plantation, Soil Protection, Organic Farming, and Agro-Logistics

Learn More

Objectives & Scope

Project Objectives

  • To promote soil protection, biodiversity, and organic farming.
  • To connect farmers with agriculture officers for expert advice.

Project Scope

The system will manage awareness program schedules, crop promotion guidelines, and basic logistics tracking for local farmers.

Organization Profile

Godavari Krishi Vikas Foundation

Services & Activities: Conducting field visits, testing soil, distributing organic seeds, and organizing farmer seminars.

Methodology for Data Collection:

  • Surveys: Questionnaires distributed to local farmers regarding current crop issues.
  • Interviews: Discussions with agriculture officers on best practices.
  • Observation: Analyzing existing manual methods for supply chain and sales.

Platform Features

Farmer Dashboard

View upcoming awareness programs and daily crop prices.

Officer Portal

Schedule seminars and upload soil protection guidelines.

Logistics Tracking

A simple screen to track agro-product shipments.

Conclusion & Future Scope

Conclusion: Digitizing the connection between farmers and experts significantly improves the adoption of organic farming and local crop promotion.

Future Enhancements

  • Integrating IoT for live soil moisture tracking.
  • Adding an e-commerce module for direct-to-consumer sales.
